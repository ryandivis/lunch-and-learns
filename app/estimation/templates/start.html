Explanation and Examples

Estimating is a big part of what we do at SolutionStream. Whether we are estimating a simple task or an entire project. This lunch and learn is designed to help us get better at estimating and architecting solutions for our clients.

Determining Scope

  • Technical Details
    • What is currently in place?
    • What needs to be added?
  • Dependencies
    • Do we need more information in order to proceed?
    • Are there components that must be finished before we begin?
    • In what order would we need to start?
  • Business Requirements
    • Functional Requirements
    • Non-Functional Requirements
  • Acceptance Criteria

Groundrules and Assumptions

  • Has the salesman already promised an amount? or has money been talked about?
  • Clearly define the scope
    • Call out items that we won't be able to handle
  • Which technologies are we going to use?
  • Could we break down the task into pieces that a non developer could handle?

Size

  • How long will the project take?
  • It is easier to talk about the project or task in man hours.
  • How many people do you need for the project?
  • Could we fit more people and do the project faster?

Risk Assessment

  • Which parts of the task/project have a heavy reliance on something else?
  • Which parts of the task/project require our assumptions to be true?
  • Which parts of the task/project could be huge black holes?

Deliver Estimate

  • Should be thinking in terms of man hours
